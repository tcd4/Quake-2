Chivalry Modification for Quake 2

-added 4 status effects
    -ensnare 
        -used by grenades/grenade launcher
        -prevents a player from moving
    -poison 
        -used by machine/chain guns
        -damages player over time
    -shock 
        -used by bfg
        -if a player moves, they will lose health
    -shake 
        -used by shotgun
        -the player's view will shake sporadically

-blocking
    -added a shield to the game
    -must bind the "block" command
    -shield blocks damage but disallows attacking during use
    -shield is togglable

-weapons are no longer long ranged
    -guns have very short attack ranges
